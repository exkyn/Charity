Our MISSION is to provide assistance to the disadvantaged and to nurture health compromised people back to health through a support system where a secure life is the priority.
TD Foundation will be staffed by doctors, physical therapists and clinicians with top qualifications in their respective fields. Driven by empathy, compassion, positivity and professionalism, our model is sustained by funds generated through donations by kind individuals who wish to make the world a better place.
Our VISION is to create a broad reach to lift people during challenging times. We are a charity that focuses on providing aid to orphans, individuals with infirmities, internally displaced persons, impoverished women, children, and athletes afflicted by injuries and maladies.
We espouse the tenets of maintaining good health, reversing ailing health, and providing an ever-present guiding hand to maintain vitality, confidence and balance in life. Reassurance is the motto. A rewarding life is the goal.
